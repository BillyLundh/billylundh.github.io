Klinisk Handledning

Klinisk Handledning is a mobile application developed to support medical students during their clinical education. The goal was to provide quick access to guidance models, structured workflows, and tools used during patient interactions.

Clinical Guidance Models

The app includes multiple clinical guidance models that help students structure their thinking during patient assessments. These models are presented in a clear and accessible way, making them easy to reference in real-world situations.

Forms & Patient Surveys

A key feature is the integration of forms used during patient surveys. Students can quickly access and use these tools directly in the app, improving efficiency and reducing the need for external materials.

User Experience & Accessibility

The application was designed with a strong focus on usability, ensuring that information can be accessed quickly in time-sensitive environments such as clinics and hospitals.

Purpose & Impact

By centralizing guidance models and tools, the app helps students feel more confident and prepared during clinical practice, supporting both learning and real-world application.

Tech Stack

  • Swift (iOS development)
  • Firebase (data storage)
  • Custom UI components
