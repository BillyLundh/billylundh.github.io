Intervjuer iOS app.

iBeacon Weather app

An experimental ibeacon app that tests monitoring mode. The idea is to have a beacon in the hallway and get notified about the weather on my way out.

I also managed to solve parsing data in a notification, which I haven't seen before. It is very useful, because this means you don't have to load up the app and get the relevant information straight away. When you want it.

Café Knus Coffee card

A digital coffee card created for a café in Malmö. The app let's users save up and get the 10th cup for free. Special timed offers are also offered in the app.

As the winners of a local hackathon hosted at Minc, me and two friends got 2 months of office time and a small sum of money to develop a concept that we conceived at the event. Our concept was a smart hoodie, that would sense your pulse and the environment around you. Emf exposure and wifi signals. The hoodie does that and converts the sensors input to a responsive music program.

A arduino talks to a raspberry pi which has a script that runs pure data of the inputs and outputs music. I programmed this script and made the responsive music track.
